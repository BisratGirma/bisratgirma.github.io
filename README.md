# Privacy Policy — Gugusha

**Last updated:** July 3, 2026

Gugusha ("the app," "we," "our") is an English learning app for Amharic speakers. This Privacy Policy explains what information the app handles, how it is used, and your choices regarding that information.

By using Gugusha, you agree to the practices described in this policy.

---

## 1. Summary

Gugusha is designed to work **entirely on your device**. We do not require you to create an account, and we do not collect, store, or transmit any personally identifiable information to our servers — because we don't operate a backend server at all.

| | |
|---|---|
| **Account required?** | No |
| **Personal data collected?** | No |
| **Data shared with third parties?** | No |
| **Data stored on your device only?** | Yes |
| **Internet access used for?** | Downloading lesson content updates only |

---

## 2. Information We Do NOT Collect

Gugusha does not collect, request, or store:

- Your name, email address, or phone number
- Location data
- Device identifiers used for tracking or advertising
- Photos, contacts, or files on your device
- Payment information
- Any data tied to your identity

There is no login system, no user profile, and no server-side database of any kind.

---

## 3. Information Stored Locally On Your Device

To let the app remember your progress, Gugusha stores the following information **locally on your device only**, using Android's standard `SharedPreferences` storage:

- Your lesson progress (which lessons you've unlocked and completed)
- Your XP (experience points)
- Your streak count
- Your accuracy statistics (questions answered and answered correctly)

**This data never leaves your device.** It is not transmitted to us, to Google, or to any third party. Uninstalling the app permanently deletes this data.

---

## 4. Content Updates (Network Access)

On each app launch, Gugusha makes a network request to a public content delivery source (CDN / GitHub Gist) to check for updated lesson content. This request:

- Downloads lesson/curriculum text only
- Does **not** send any of your personal information, progress, or device identifiers
- Is used solely to keep the app's learning content up to date
- Runs silently in the background; updated content is applied the next time you open the app

No user data is included in or generated by this request beyond standard, anonymous network request metadata (e.g., IP address), which is inherent to any internet connection and is not collected, logged, or used by us.

---

## 5. Analytics

Gugusha's codebase currently includes internal event *hooks* (such as `lesson_started`, `lesson_completed`, and `user_dropped_out`) intended for a future analytics integration. **As of this version, these hooks are not connected to any analytics service, and no analytics or usage data is collected or transmitted.**

If analytics are enabled in a future update, this policy will be updated in advance to disclose:
- What is collected
- Which service is used
- How to opt out, if applicable

---

## 6. Third-Party Services

Gugusha does not integrate any third-party SDKs that collect user data, including:

- No advertising networks
- No analytics SDKs (currently)
- No crash reporting services
- No social login providers

The only external network calls are anonymous content fetches described in Section 4.

---

## 7. Children's Privacy

Gugusha does not knowingly collect any personal information from anyone, including children under 13. Since the app requires no account and no personal data to function, it is safe for use by learners of all ages.

---

## 8. Data Security

Since no personal data is transmitted to or stored on any server we control, there is no server-side data for us to secure. Data stored locally on your device is protected by your device's own operating system security.

---

## 9. Your Choices

- **Delete your data:** Uninstall the app, or clear the app's storage/cache via your device settings.
- **Offline use:** You may disable network access for the app; it will continue to function using the bundled lesson content, though it will not receive content updates.

---

We may update this Privacy Policy as the app evolves (for example, if analytics are added in a future release). Material changes will be reflected in this document with an updated "Last updated" date. Continued use of the app after changes take effect constitutes acceptance of the revised policy.
